How to Indroduce in Yourself in Job Interview?

In a job interview, introducing yourself effectively is essential. Understand the company culture and values. Start with a friendly greeting and state your name confidently. Give a brief overview of your professional background, emphasizing relevant experience and skills. Keep it concise and focused to make a strong initial impression.
