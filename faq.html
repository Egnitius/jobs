Frequently Asked Questions

How To Find A Job?

all the jobs on this site can be searched for via the search option. Or you can browse through the list and select the one you want.

How Many Companies Can I Applied?

The application process is not restricting in that you can apply to a multitude of companies but only apply to a single company once for that role.

How Can I Applied for Job?

Its simple since all yo have to do is click on the apply button for the job you have selected.

How can I Pay?

Payment is only restricted to recruiters since they post up job opportunities to be used by the user, that is you.

How Can I Connect with a Company?

Every company on our site gives their contact details for their section. So it is simple to just pick up you phone and calling. Also their emails are available.

How Can I Create an Account?

All you do is on the navigation bar at the top of the website click on sign up and if you are a candidate please select the candidate option. If you are a recruiter please select the recruiter option.
